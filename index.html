Biography

I am a 5th-year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University (THU) from 2017, supervised by Prof. Xiu Li. I also received B.Eng in Department of Automation and B.Sc in Management from THU in 2017.

Thanks for Prof. Li's supports, I acted as a research leader for Tsinghua Intelligence Computation Lab and found the Medical Image Analysis Student Research Group and co-found Reinforcement Learning Student Research Group.

I was a reseach intern supervised by Dr. Qiong Yan at SenseTime in 2018. I joined Tencent AI Lab as a research intern in 2020, working closely with Dr. Hanbo Chen and was supervised by the chief scientist Dr. Jianhua Yao.

Personally, my research interest lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare. Because I lead two student research groups, I participate in multiple research topics thanks to my co-authors' trust.
